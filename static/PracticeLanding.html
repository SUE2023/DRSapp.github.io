About

Having worked in a startup with no central records, unrecorded work processes, and reduced profitability due to these issues, the directors realized the need to market the company's services. Faced with this situation, I volunteered to head the marketing department with a desire to solve these problems, onboard salespeople and planned to provide them with intense training for 16 months, combining theory and fieldwork. This required a system to track progress. DRSapp is intended to solve this problem and assist startups with a comprehensive solution.

The project is carried out within 10 weeks starting from 24th May 2024 to 10th July 2024 when marketing for the user trials continues, being a Portfolio Project for AlxSe/Holberton School learing project.
